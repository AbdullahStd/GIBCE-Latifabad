WELCOME TO GIBCE

INTRODUCTION

Vocational & Technical Education has historically been known as "Education for work". It has focused on providing learners with the skills and Knowledge needed to successfully transition to the workplace. Pakistan with a labour surplus economy,on one side has rapidly increasing unemployed youth while on the other end its public and private sector employers desperately look for technicaly educated and trained workforce.The shortage of skilled labour is due to a mismatch between academic programmes and market oriented skills and trainings.To meet these challanges , "Sindh TEVTA" , like global best practices, is attending to these issues through improving efficiency of TEVT administration and introducing market oriented academic programmes,training,and developing industrial linkage.

MESSAGE

"The goal of Education is understanding,
The goal of training is performance"

Future of Pakistan is linked with our capability of imparting quality and market oriented skils to our youth for employbility through technical education & vocational training.With this vission "Sindh TEVTA" has been endeavouring to increase the awarness level to attract more and more of our youth towords"Technical Education & Vocational Training".This is we are trying to achieve by improving our programmes Institutional infrastructure and transparency in admission process. After successful centralized admission policy "Sindh TEVTA" has introduced transparent admissions through a recognized 3rd party testing service since 2010.This has given more credence to our admission process and made our graduates relatively more acceptable to the employers.
